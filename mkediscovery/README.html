MkEDiscovery

When Ethernet and TCP connections are supported by a sensor, the sensor can also optionally implement SSDP (Simple Service Discovery Protocol) for advertisement and discovery. MkEDiscovery is a multi-platform GUI utility that lists names and IP adresseses of Magik Eye sensors supporting SSDP discovery on the local network. It is based on the Qt toolkit and the libmkeclient library.
